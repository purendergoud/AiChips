About Us

One way to think of zen is this: a total state of focus that incorporates a total togetherness of body and mind. Zen is a way of being. It also is a state of mind ( https://www.urbandictionary.com)

Zensark tries to bring these same principles of zen to building software

Zensark is a technology services company with a footprint in the US and India. Zensark brings over a decade of experience in software development, integration, strategy development, and deployment. Zensark started its journey by building healthcare related software and services. In the last 18 months, aligning to changing business landscape and new opportunities, Zensark has forayed into solving new age business problems in fields such as Voice Analytics, Conversational AI and Video Interviews.

Zensark has rich experience in product engineering consulting for startups and SMBs, taking them from idea to POC to Prototype to Product. We specialize in the early perception of business use cases and potential applications of new technology. We offer custom application development as well as production support for existing solutions.

our mission

Our mission is to enhance the business growth of our customers with creative design and development, by delivering market defining high-quality solutions that create value and competitive advantage for our clients around the world.  We want to be the preferred technology partner for small and medium enterprises by offering technology consulting, outsourced product engineering and product support services by leveraging our deep expertise across emerging technologies, Cloud based development and building software for Enterprise scale.

Our Core Values
Customer First
We strive very hard to ensure happy customers. Satisfied and happy customers who love to use the products we build are the best way to grow our business. We prioritize Collaboration over Contractual agreements.
Innovate
We love to challenge the status-quo. Our R&D team is continuously evaluating new technology that is exciting & useful while at the same time, stable enough to solve real business problems.
Efficient Simplicity
We focus on building optimal, minimally engineered & Intuitive solutions . Even the most complex problems can be solved by a simple and easy to use solution.
Agility
We focus on Incrementally building software while validating what we are building & keeping stakeholders closely connected with the progress with demos. Our Agile practices are well suited to aggressive timelines.
